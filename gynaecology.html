Gynaecology Treatment Services

At Sterling Hospital, we offer comprehensive gynaecological care including routine check-ups, diagnostic services, and specialized treatments for a wide range of conditions affecting women's reproductive health. Our experienced team is dedicated to providing personalized and compassionate care.

We provide the following gynaecology treatment services:

  • Routine Check-ups: Regular health check-ups for early detection and prevention of gynaecological issues.
  • Diagnostic Services: Advanced diagnostic tools for accurate identification of gynaecological conditions.
  • Minimally Invasive Surgery: State-of-the-art minimally invasive surgical techniques for faster recovery.
  • Infertility Treatment: Comprehensive infertility treatments tailored to individual needs.
  • Pelvic Pain Management: Effective management and treatment of chronic pelvic pain.
  • Menopause Care: Comprehensive care and support for women going through menopause.

Our team of gynaecologists is highly experienced and committed to providing the best possible care to our patients. We ensure that each patient receives personalized treatment and support throughout their journey to better health.

Routine Check-ups

Regular health check-ups for early detection and prevention of gynaecological issues. Our expert gynaecologists perform thorough examinations and provide personalized care to ensure optimal health.

  • Pap Smear
  • Breast Examination
  • Pelvic Examination
  • General Health Assessment
Diagnostic Services

Advanced diagnostic tools for accurate identification of gynaecological conditions. Our diagnostic services include imaging tests, blood tests, and specialized procedures for comprehensive evaluation.

  • Ultrasound
  • MRI
  • Hysteroscopy
  • Biopsy
Minimally Invasive Surgery

State-of-the-art minimally invasive surgical techniques for faster recovery. Our gynaecological experts perform advanced procedures using cutting-edge technology to ensure minimal discomfort and quick recovery.

  • Laparoscopy
  • Hysteroscopy
  • Laser Surgery
  • Robotic Surgery
Infertility Treatment

Comprehensive infertility treatments tailored to individual needs. Our fertility specialists offer a range of assisted reproductive technologies (ART) to help couples achieve their dream of parenthood.

  • In Vitro Fertilization (IVF)
  • Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI)
  • Egg Donation
  • Surrogacy
Pelvic Pain Management

Effective management and treatment of chronic pelvic pain. Our specialists use a multidisciplinary approach, including medication, physical therapy, and minimally invasive procedures, to alleviate pelvic pain and improve quality of life.

  • Pain Medication
  • Physical Therapy
  • Trigger Point Injections
  • Nerve Blocks
Menopause Care

Comprehensive care and support for women going through menopause. Our experienced gynaecologists provide personalized treatment plans to manage symptoms and improve overall well-being during this life stage.

  • Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT)
  • Nutritional Counseling
  • Exercise Recommendations
  • Emotional Support
