Gynaecology Treatment Services

At Sterling Hospital, we offer comprehensive gynaecological care including routine check-ups, diagnostic services, and specialized treatments for a wide range of conditions affecting women's reproductive health. Our experienced team is dedicated to providing personalized and compassionate care.

We provide the following gynaecology treatment services:

  • Routine Gynecological Exams.
  • Menstrual Disorders Treatment.
  • Gynaecological Surgery.
  • Gynecological Cancer Screening and Treatment.
  • Adolescent Gynecology.
  • Contraception and Family Planning.
  • Menopause Management.
  • PCOS clinic.

Our team of gynaecologists is highly experienced and committed to providing the best possible care to our patients. We ensure that each patient receives personalized treatment and support throughout their journey to better health.

Routine Gynecological Exams

Regular health check-ups for early detection and prevention of gynecological issues. Our expert gynecologists perform thorough examinations and provide personalized care to ensure optimal health and address all individual concerns.

  • Pap Smear
  • Breast Examination
  • Pelvic Examination
  • General Health Assessment
Menstrual Disorders Treatment

Routine gynecological exams are essential for maintaining reproductive health and detecting potential issues early. These exams include a series of evaluations such as Pap smears, breast examinations, and pelvic exams, which help in diagnosing and preventing various gynecological conditions.

  • Annual pelvic examinations
  • Pap smears and HPV testing
  • Breast examinations
Gynaecological Surgery

We offer minimally invasive gynecological surgeries for fibroids, cysts, and endometriosis, including laparoscopy, and hysteroscopy.

  • Minimally invasive laparoscopic surgery
  • Hysterectomy
  • Myomectomy (fibroid removal)
  • Ovarian cyst removal
  • Endometriosis treatment
  • Endometrial biopsy
Gynecological Cancer Screening and Treatment

We provide comprehensive screening and treatment for gynecological cancers. Our specialized services include tailored infertility treatments using assisted reproductive technologies (ART) like IVF, ICSI, egg donation, and surrogacy.

  • Screening for cervical, ovarian, and uterine cancers
  • Colposcopy and biopsy
  • Treatment planning and management
Adolescent Gynecology

Our adolescent gynecology services focus on effective management and treatment of chronic pelvic pain. Our specialists use a range of approaches, including medication, physical therapy, trigger point injections, and nerve blocks, to alleviate pain and improve quality of life.

  • Education on reproductive health
  • Management of menstrual disorders
  • HPV vaccination
  • Managing PCOS
Contraception and Family Planning

Our contraception and family planning services include comprehensive care and support for women. We offer personalized treatment plans, including hormone replacement therapy (HRT), nutritional counseling, exercise recommendations, and emotional support.

  • Birth control counseling and prescriptions
  • IUD insertion and removal
Menopause Management

Comprehensive care and support for women going through menopause. Our experienced gynaecologists provide personalized treatment plans to manage symptoms and improve overall well-being during this life stage.

  • Hormone replacement therapy (HRT)
  • Management of menopausal symptoms
  • Osteoporosis screening and treatment
PCOS clinic

Our PCOS clinic provides comprehensive care and support for women with polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS). Our experienced gynaecologists offer personalized treatment plans, including hormone management, lifestyle adjustments, fertility guidance, and emotional support, to manage symptoms effectively and improve overall well-being.

  • Diagnosis and management of PCOS
