Dr. Sachin Zambare

DNB orthopaedics

Fellowship in Adult and Paediatric Spine Surgery (Europe) Fellowship in Onco-Spine Surgery ( Europe)

Dr Sachin Zambare is a highly skilled Consultant Orthopaedic and Spine Surgeon with over 15 years of experience , specializing in spine surgeries and trauma care. With extensive training in Europe ,he holds fellowships in Adult & Paediatric Spine Surgery and Onco-Spine Surgery, making him proficient in a broad range of spinal conditions and treatments. Dr. Zambare is dedicated to providing comprehensive and individualized care for each patient, combining advanced surgical techniques with a compassionate approach. His expertise ensures the highest standards of precision and outcomes in spine surgery . Dr. Zambare is committed to delivering exceptional orthopedic and spinal care, ensuring his patients achieve the best possible recovery and quality of life.
